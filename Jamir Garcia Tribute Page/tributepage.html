Vladimir "Jamir" Salenga Garcia

Vocalist of Slapshock

1978 - 2020





About


Jamir Garcia was born on September 5, 1978 in the Philippines as Vladimir Salenga Garcia. He was an actor and composer, known for Tribu (2007), Slapshock: Agent Orange (1999) and Slapshock: Get Away (2001). He was married to Sojina Jaya. He died on November 26, 2020 in Quezon City, Philippines.[1]
